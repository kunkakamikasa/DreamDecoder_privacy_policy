Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

DreamDecoder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application DreamDecoder (the "App").

This App processes dream interpretation data primarily locally on your device. Certain features require network connectivity for advertising and analytics purposes.

2. Device Identifiers and Information

We collect the following identifiers and device information:

  • Google Advertising ID (GAID): Collected by advertising SDKs for ad targeting and analytics. You can reset or opt out through your device settings.
  • Device identifiers: Device model, operating system version, app version for functionality and analytics.
  • Application instance identifier: Anonymous identifier generated by Firebase Analytics for user session tracking.

3. Runtime Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for displaying advertisements and sending anonymous analytics data.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to determine network availability for ad loading.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+): Required for sharing dream interpretation cards as images.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below): Required for accessing saved dream cards.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below): Required for saving dream cards to device storage.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. Note that revoking permissions may affect app functionality.

4. Data Categories and Purposes

4.1 Data We Collect

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data including app launches, screen views, ad interactions, feature usage.
  • Device Data: Device type, OS version, app version for compatibility and debugging.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ad impressions, clicks, conversions collected by advertising SDKs for ad serving and optimization.
  • Crash and Performance Data: Error logs, crash reports, performance metrics for app stability and improvement.
  • Local Dream Records: Dream keywords, interpretations, and analysis history stored locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to servers.

4.2 How We Use Your Data

  • App Functionality: Processing dream interpretations locally, storing your dream history on device.
  • Advertising: Displaying relevant advertisements, ad frequency capping, ad optimization.
  • Analytics: Understanding app usage patterns, improving features, measuring performance.
  • Crash Reporting: Identifying and fixing app crashes and errors.
  • Performance Monitoring: Measuring app performance and optimizing load times.

5. Data Processing Location and Association

5.1 Local Processing

The following data is processed and stored locally on your device:

  • Dream interpretation results
  • Dream history records (keyword, meaning, psychology analysis)
  • Generated dream cards (images)

This locally stored data is not associated with your identity and is not uploaded to our servers or third parties.

5.2 Data Upload

The following anonymous data is transmitted to third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage events (app opens, screen views, feature interactions)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports and stack traces (may contain device identifiers)
  • Firebase Performance: Performance metrics (app startup time, screen load times)
  • Advertising SDKs: Ad identifiers, ad interaction data, device information for ad targeting

5.3 Personal Identification

We do not link collected data to personally identifiable information. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. We do not use data for cross-app tracking beyond advertising purposes permitted by third-party SDKs.

7. Consent and User Controls

7.1 GDPR Compliance (EEA/UK Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent for personalized advertising. You can:

  • Choose to consent to personalized ads or non-personalized ads
  • Withdraw consent at any time through app settings or device advertising settings
  • Reset your advertising ID through device settings

7.2 iOS ATT (App Tracking Transparency)

This App is Android-only. iOS tracking transparency does not apply.

7.3 Advertising Controls

You can manage advertising preferences:

  • Reset Advertising ID: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Opt out of personalized ads: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Ad consent: Manage through in-app consent dialog (EEA/UK users)

7.4 Analytics Controls

You can disable Firebase Analytics data collection by uninstalling the App or through device settings, though this may impact app functionality.

8. Data Sharing, Retention, and Security

8.1 Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • Advertising partners: Ad identifiers, device information for ad serving (TopOn, AdMob, Meta, Unity, Pangle)
  • Analytics providers: Anonymous usage data (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reporting services: Crash logs and device information (Firebase Crashlytics)

We do not sell your personal data. Data sharing is limited to service provision and advertising.

8.2 Data Retention

  • Local dream records: Stored on your device until you delete the App or manually clear app data. Maximum 100 records retained locally.
  • Analytics data: Retained by Firebase Analytics according to Google's retention policies (typically 14 months).
  • Crash reports: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days.
  • Advertising data: Retained by advertising SDKs according to their respective policies.

8.3 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Local data encryption: Dream records stored in app's private directory with Android sandbox protection
  • Network security: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Minimal permissions: App requests only necessary permissions for functionality
  • No personal data transmission: Dream interpretation content is processed locally and not transmitted
